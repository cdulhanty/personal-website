Chris Dulhanty

Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Apple

I'm a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Apple in New York City, where I work to anticipate, evaluate, and mitigate the technical and social failures of machine learning systems.

I completed my M.A.Sc. in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo in 2020 as a member of the Vision and Image Processing Lab, supervised by Alexander Wong and David Clausi. Before that, I earned a B.Eng. in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Guelph in 2015.

My research interests sit at the intersection of computer vision, datasets, data collection, and fairness — in particular, how the choices we make when assembling training data shape the behavior and failure modes of the models we deploy.
